WHAT IS TOOLUCS?

An Open System For Territory Utility

Toolucs allows individuals to list and exchange goods, services, skills and cash based on location proximity and manual search.

The Mission

The average neighborhood operates at less than 10% of its potential. Over 90% of tools sit idle behind closed doors. Toolucs exists to stop this waste and activate territory-level efficiency.

The Philosophy

Before humanity becomes a galactic civilization, it must first learn to utilize its immediate surroundings. Toolucs begins at the room, building and society level.
