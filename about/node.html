Defining The Node

Pune city, Maharashtra - India

A Toolucs Node is a high-density urban cluster (e.g., Pune) representing a massive reservoir of latent utility. Each node contains thousands of high-value tools, skills, and resources currently locked behind closed doors. Toolucs is the key to activating this trapped energy.

The Probability Law: Option Density

Unlike standard marketplaces, Toolucs operates on the principle of Option Density . Our architecture is engineered to maximize the probability of a superior exchange.

"The maximum the number of users in an area, The higher the probability of getting better options."

System Status: Node-01 [Pune]

To maintain peak response velocity and resource depth, the mesh is currently restricted to Node-01: Pune (Kothrud, Baner, Vimanagar, etc.)..

Full system activation follows a strict threephase deployment:

Phase 1 [Node-01 Activation]: Territories remain in "Staged" mode until 50% of the local population equips the protocol. This is the threshold required to move from TPS 0.1 to TPS 0.5.

Phase 2 [Provincial Scaling]: Upon peak saturation of Node-01, the system will initialize Node-02, targeting subsequent highutility cities in Maharashtra.

Phase 3 [Territorial Integration]:Sequential activation of Nodes 03, 04, and 05 continues until the mesh covers the entire Maharashtra territory, eventually expanding to unify the national resource grid.

Direct Exchange Protocol: Zero-friction negotiation. Goods, services, or cash settled entirely on your terms—no middlemen, no fees.

The 48-Hour Pulse: A high-velocity network. All signals expire after 48 hours to ensure a zero-stagnation, real-time environment.>

Merit-Based Trust: Build your standing within the mesh. Every verified interaction strengthens node reliability and individual self-reliance.
